### Key Takeaways

- **Worlds** is the only major world management plugin with **Folia support** and built-in **world presets**
- **Multiverse-Core** has the largest user base but requires separate addon plugins for portal linking
- **MyWorlds** is feature-rich but requires BKCommonLib as a dependency
